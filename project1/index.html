How it works

As a host, it's easy to create your party: add the names, click Draw, then send out the party link. All of the Santas will remain secret!

If you'd like, Create a Party and all the details will be available to each Santa when they join from their party link.

Create public or private wishlists, and anonymously request wishlists from the person whose name you drew.
